WE ARE THE WHALES!

Troop 936 is a great troop. We do lots of fun activities such as hikes, campouts, service and much more! Our troop is based out of Livermore, California in the San Francisco bay area. Our troop is divided into three patrols click on the patrols tab to learn more. Our chartering organization is The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints
