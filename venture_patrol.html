Whale Venture Patrol

The Venture patrol is for ages 16-18. Meetings are regularly scheduled for Wednesday evenings at 7 p.m., but meeting times can vary depending on troop members school and extracurricular activities. Many of the activities are focused on serving in the community, supporting troop members in the completion of their Eagle rank, learning life-skills, and building spiritual, mental, and physical strength. Venturers in 936 often plan their own campouts but sometimes join the younger scouts. The Venture Patrol also does high adventure outings that are a lot of fun.
